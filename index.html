Gemstone Collector Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four gemstones below. By clicking on each of the gemstones you will add a specific amount of points to your original score.

You win the game by matching your score to the original number. You lose the game by going over the orginal amount.

The value of each gemstone is hidden until you click on it.

The value of the gemstones will change each time the game starts.
